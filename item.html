Black coffee is a simple yet bold beverage made from brewed coffee beans without any added milk, cream, or sugar. Its rich, intense flavor is favored by coffee enthusiasts who appreciate the pure taste of coffee.

A burger is a popular fast food item consisting of a ground beef patty served in a bun, often with a variety of toppings like cheese, lettuce, tomato, and pickles. It can be customized with endless variations, from veggie burgers to gourmet creations.

Pasta is a traditional Italian dish made from durum wheat and water, typically shaped into various forms like spaghetti, penne, or fusilli. It can be served with a wide range of sauces, from classic marinara to rich Alfredo or pesto.

Milk coffee is a smooth, creamy beverage made by combining brewed coffee with steamed or frothed milk. The milk softens the coffee's boldness, creating a balanced, rich flavor that's popular in lattes and cappuccinos.
